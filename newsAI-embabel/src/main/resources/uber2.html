Uber Expands Logistics Platform with New Enterprise Features

Uber has announced the expansion of its logistics platform to support enterprise customers with advanced supply chain capabilities.

The new platform enables businesses to manage bulk shipments, track real-time deliveries, and optimize routing using AI-driven analytics.

According to company executives, the move positions Uber as a strong competitor in the B2B logistics and freight market.

Industry analysts believe this expansion could significantly increase Uber's revenue from non-ride-sharing services over the next few years.

The rollout will begin in North America before expanding globally.
